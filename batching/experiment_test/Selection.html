Key Instructions

For the purpose of this task, you are given a numerical score of 80.00 to be used to apply for university courses for the year of 2024. You will be made an offer to a course if your score is higher than the LSS for 2024, which is not known to you currently.

Based on your preferences, a process will determine whether you are made an offer to a course. This process takes the first-preferred course on your preference list and:

  • if you are made an offer to your first-preferred course, you will then be paid the associated monetary payoff.
  • if you are not made an offer, you are then considered for your second-preferred course.

This process continues until all five preferences have been exhausted. If you are made an offer to a university course, you will be paid the associated monetary amount as a bonus payment in addition to your participation fee of Â£1.50.

The further below that your score of 80.00 is from the 2023 LSS, the lesser the chance of being made an offer. Conversely, the further above that your score is from the 2023 LSS, the greater the chance of being made an offer.
